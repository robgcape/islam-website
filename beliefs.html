The foundation of Islamic faith and daily spiritual life.

The Five Pillars of Islam

The Five Pillars form the core of Muslim belief and practice. They guide worship, community responsibility, and spiritual discipline.

Shahada (Faith)

The declaration that there is no god but Allah and Muhammad is His messenger.

Salah (Prayer)

Five daily prayers that structure a Muslim’s day and strengthen their connection to God.

Zakat (Charity)

Giving a portion of one’s wealth to support those in need and uplift the community.

Sawm (Fasting)

Fasting during Ramadan to develop self-discipline, empathy, and spiritual focus.

Hajj (Pilgrimage)

A sacred journey to Mecca required once in a lifetime for those able to perform it.
