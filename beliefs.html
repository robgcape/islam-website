Understanding the Five Pillars and daily practices of Islam.

The Five Pillars of Islam

Shahada (Faith)

The declaration of faith, expressing belief in one God.

Salah (Prayer)

Performing ritual prayers five times a day to maintain a connection with God.

Zakat (Charity)

Giving to the poor and supporting the community through almsgiving.

Sawm (Fasting)

Fasting during the month of Ramadan to practice self-discipline and empathy.

Hajj (Pilgrimage)

Making a pilgrimage to Mecca at least once in a lifetime if physically and financially able.
