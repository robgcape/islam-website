Revelation

The Qur'an was revealed to Prophet Muhammad over a period of 23 years through the Angel Gabriel.

Structure of the Qur'an

The Qur'an contains 114 chapters called Surahs and is written in classical Arabic.

Importance in Muslim Life

Muslims believe the Qur'an provides spiritual, moral, and legal guidance for all aspects of life.
