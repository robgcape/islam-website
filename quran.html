The Holy Qur'an

The divine revelation that guides the lives of over a billion Muslims worldwide.

About the Qur'an

The Qur'an is the central religious text of Islam, believed to be the literal word of God revealed to the Prophet Muhammad over 23 years. It provides guidance on faith, morality, law, and personal conduct.

Key Concepts

Revelation

The Qur'an was revealed through the Angel Gabriel, beginning in the year 610 CE.

Structure

It contains 114 chapters (Surahs) and is written in classical Arabic.

Role in Daily Life

Muslims recite, memorize, and reflect on the Qur'an as a source of spiritual guidance.
